AI Data Usage Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

1. Introduction

These AI Data Usage & Policies govern how Webropol Oy ("Webropol", "we", "us") collects, processes, and uses data in connection with our artificial intelligence features. By enabling AI features within your Webropol account, your organization agrees to the terms set out in this policy. Please read this document carefully before accepting.

2. Data We Process

When you use AI-powered features, Webropol may process the following categories of data:

  • Survey questions, answer options, and structure you have created or imported.
  • Aggregated and anonymized response data used to generate AI-driven insights and recommendations.
  • Organization context information you have provided voluntarily in Admin Tools settings.
  • Usage metadata (feature interactions, timestamps) to improve AI model quality over time.

We do not use individual respondent personally identifiable information (PII) to train AI models without explicit, separately obtained consent.

3. Purpose of Processing

Data is processed solely for the following purposes:

  • Generating survey question suggestions tailored to your organization's context.
  • Producing automated analytics summaries and trend insights.
  • Optimizing email and SMS distribution timing and targeting.
  • Predicting event attendance and engagement patterns.
  • Continuously improving the accuracy and relevance of AI recommendations.

4. Data Retention

AI-processed data is retained for the duration of your subscription. Upon account termination or explicit deletion request, all AI-related data will be purged within 30 calendar days in accordance with applicable data protection regulations, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Finnish Data Protection Act.

5. Third-Party AI Providers

Webropol may rely on vetted third-party AI infrastructure providers for model inference. All such providers are contractually bound to process data exclusively on our behalf, maintain equivalent security standards, and refrain from using your data for their own training or commercial purposes. A current list of sub-processors is available upon request.

6. Security & Access Controls

Access to AI-processed data is restricted to authorized Webropol personnel on a need-to-know basis. All data transmissions are encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). We conduct periodic security audits and penetration tests of our AI infrastructure. Administrators may disable AI features at any time from this settings panel.

7. Your Rights

As a data controller, your organization retains full ownership of survey content and response data. You have the right to:

  • Access a copy of data processed by AI features.
  • Request correction or deletion of AI-derived insights.
  • Withdraw consent and disable AI features without penalty.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman (tietosuoja.fi).

8. Policy Updates

Webropol reserves the right to update this policy to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or business practices. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notification and email to your organization's administrator at least 30 days before taking effect. Continued use of AI features after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For questions or concerns regarding this policy, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@webropol.com or write to Webropol Oy, Tekniikantie 14, 02150 Espoo, Finland.
