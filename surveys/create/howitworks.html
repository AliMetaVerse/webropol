Create a survey – step-by-step

A quick visual guide to the flow

Give a Name

Start by naming your survey and choosing a folder.

Add Template

Pick a layout template from Webropol, My, or Shared.

Create

Click Create to scaffold your survey with the selected template.

Design

Design and build your survey: add questions and sections, configure logic and rules, and fine‑tune the look & feel.

  • Add questions, groups, and sections
  • Set branching/skip/display logic
  • Apply validation and required rules
  • Customize theme, colors, and transparency

Publish

Share your survey via link, email, or QR code.

Follow up

Schedule reminders to boost response rates.

Get Report

Monitor results in real-time and export insights.

AI Text Analysis

Use AI to categorize feedback, detect sentiment, and surface themes.

Tip: You can return to this guide any time from the Create page.
