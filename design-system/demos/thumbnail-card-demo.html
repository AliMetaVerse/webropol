ThumbnailCard

Selectable thumbnail card for template or theme pickers. Shows a miniature preview via animated bars, supports selected and disabled states.

States

Use selected to mark the current choice and disabled to make a card non-selectable. The preview-bars attribute generates a miniature bar chart in the preview area.

Attributes

titleLabel below the preview area
preview-barsComma-separated percentages (0–100) used to draw the mini bar chart
selectedBoolean — shows a checkmark overlay and highlight border
disabledBoolean — mutes the card and prevents interaction
