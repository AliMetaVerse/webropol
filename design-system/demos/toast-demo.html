Toast

Ephemeral notification popups that appear in a fixed corner and auto-dismiss. Four semantic variants with matching icons and colours.

Live Demo

Click any button to trigger a toast notification. Each toast auto-dismisses after a few seconds.

Attributes

variantinfo · success · warning · error
