AI Loader

Full-screen overlay used while an AI flow is generating content (AI Survey Creator, AI Question Generator, AI Reports). Pairs an animated sparkles badge, a gradient progress bar that drives an auto-advancing step caption, and shimmering skeleton previews of the result.

AI Overlay ARIA live

Live preview

Click Play simulation to drive progress from 0 to 100%. The step caption advances automatically across the four steps.

Inline variant

Add inline to render the loader inside its parent box instead of a fixed overlay — useful for in-card or in-panel AI generations.

Usage

Bind active and progress to your Alpine state. The component handles its own animations, ARIA state, and step calculation.

<script src="../design-system/components/feedback/AiLoader.js" type="module"></script>

<webropol-ai-loader
  :active="isGenerating"
  :progress="generationProgress"
  title="Generating your survey…"
  subtitle="AI is crafting the perfect questions"
  steps='["Analyzing your prompt…","Selecting optimal questions…","Structuring survey logic…","Finalising your survey…"]'
  skeletons="3">
</webropol-ai-loader>

API

AttributeTypeDefaultDescription
activebooleanfalseShows the overlay when present (or "true"). Hide by removing the attribute.
progressnumber 0–1000Drives the gradient progress bar and selects the active step. Updates are patched in place (no re-render flicker).
titlestring“Generating your content…”Headline shown under the badge.
subtitlestring“AI is preparing your result”Supporting copy under the headline.
stepsJSON array4 default stepsStep captions distributed evenly across 0–100% by index.
inlinebooleanfalseRenders inside the parent box instead of a fixed full-screen overlay.
skeletonsnumber3Skeleton preview rows shown beneath the progress bar. Set to 0 to hide.

When to use

Reserve this loader for AI-generated content where the wait is meaningful (typically 1.5s+) and the result will replace or fill the surface the user is looking at.

  • Do use it for AI Survey Creator, AI Question Generator, and AI Report drafting.
  • Do drive progress from real signals when available; otherwise simulate a smooth 0→88% climb and snap to 100% on completion.
  • Don’t use it for plain network requests — use <webropol-loading> spinner instead.
  • Don’t stack it over a modal; close the modal first or render the loader inside it with inline.
